Solar Home Systems

Benefits:

Reduction in electricity bills
- In monetary terms, the average savings per year with a 1 KW system will be INR 12000/-. In just 4-5 years, the system reaches its breakeven and for the next 20 years, we can get the benefits of Solar System. Energy saving Long Life- Life of a solar system is approximately 25 years.
Low maintenance
- To ensure that the panels are working on maximum efficiency, the dust on the panels need to be cleaned, which can easily be done on regular interval.
Reduction in carbon footprint
- Solar solution installation in house is the easiest way in which a household can contribute towards green environment.

Cost of Installation:

- Approximate cost of installation of a 1 KW system will be INR 40000/-
