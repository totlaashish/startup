Rain Water Harvesting

Benefits:

Reduction in water bills
- Water collected through rain water harvesting can be put to drinking and non-drinking purposes. The overall cost of installation and operation is much lesser than that of water purifying and pumping systems. For many families and businesses, this lead to reduction in utility bills and energy expenses.
Increase life of plumbing system
- Rainwater is not as hard as groundwater, making it less corrosive and damaging to the system that contains and redistributes it.
Reduce floods and soil erosion
- Rainwater harvesting helps in reducing floods in low lying areas. It also helps in reducing soil erosion and contamination of surface water, which resulted in cleaner lakes and ponds.
Reduced dependency on municipal water
- The solution makes homes and societies self-sufficient in water requirements. The dependency on municipal or other water resources reduces.
Reduce demand of Ground water
- With increase in population, the demand of ground water is continuously increasing. This has led to depletion of ground water which has gone to significantly low level.

Cost of Installation:

- The cost of installation of rain water system will be site specific. In general, the approximate cost for a 30 Sqmt roof area will be INR 30000.

Surface water harvesting solution

Benefits:

Reduce wastage of rain water
- Surface water solutions help in reducing water wastage due to run off.
Reductions in soil erosion
- Principle types of soil erosion by water are splash erosion, sheet erosion, interrill erosion, gully erosion, landslides and stream erosion. Soil Erosion lead to loss of organic matter and nutrients, damage to crops and decline in soil fertility and productivity. Surface water harvesting helps in reducing these effects of soil erosion.
