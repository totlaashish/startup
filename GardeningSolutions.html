Gardening Solutions

Rooftop garden

Benefits:

Best method to fight pollution in cities
- It is a good option to maintain the levels of PM10, nitrogen dioxide, ozone, nitrogen dioxide etc in permissible limit
Good for health
- According to a study by the University of Exeter Medical School, having regular contact with nature and green spaces in urban areas can help to keep you mentally healthy and aid with your overall happiness.
Energy efficient
- Rooftop gardens can lead to less and more effective use of energy. They provide great insulation, retaining heat in winter and keeping temperature cool in summer.
Organic farming
- Rooftop gardens in homes gives an opportunity to grow vegetables and fruits organically at home.

Cost of Installation:

- Depends upon space, types of plants and layout.

Vertical Garden

Benefits:

Reduce air pollution in cities
- According to a study, vertical gardens are best natural air filters and easy to install in urban areas.
Small space required
- For those of you who are confined to apartment lifestyle and do not have enough horizontal space for practicing full-fledged gardening, vertical gardening can be a savior.

Cost of Installation:

- The cost of vertical garden varies between INR 600 per sqft to INR 2000 per sqft, depending upon the system you chose, design, plants, irrigation system etc.
